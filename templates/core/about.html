Maedix is an Instagram automation platform that auto-replies to comments and sends follow-up DMs using keyword triggers. Built for creators, businesses, and influencers, it features a visual automation builder, quick reply buttons, follower check branching, and AI social agents. Maedix is a simpler, more affordable ManyChat alternative with a free plan that includes unlimited replies. Paid plans start at $1.99/month (Pro) and $5.99/month (Creator). Founded in 2024 and based in India.
