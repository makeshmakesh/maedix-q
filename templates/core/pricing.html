Frequently Asked Questions

A DM Automation is an automated conversation sequence. When someone comments on your post with a trigger keyword, it automatically:
  • Replies to their comment
  • Sends them a DM with your message
  • Shows interactive buttons for them to tap
  • Continues the conversation based on their responses
Example: Someone comments "LINK" on your Reel → They get a comment reply + a DM with Quick Reply buttons → They tap "Get Free Guide" → They receive your download link!

You can have 1 automation running at a time. That automation can be as complex as you want - with multiple steps, Quick Replies, Follower Checks, etc. And it handles unlimited responses!

Want to run automation on multiple posts? Upgrade to Pro (5 automations) or Creator (unlimited automations).

No! Replies are unlimited on all plans. Whether you're on Free, Pro, or Creator - every comment that triggers your automation gets the full automated experience. The only difference between plans is how many automations can be active simultaneously.

Quick Replies are tappable buttons that appear below your DM message. Users can tap them to continue the conversation - perfect for surveys, quizzes, or guiding users to different content.

Button Templates are similar but can include URL buttons that open links directly. Great for directing users to your website, Calendly, or product pages.

Follower Check lets you create different paths based on whether someone follows you or not.

Example: If they're a follower → Send exclusive discount. If not → Ask them to follow first, then send the regular offer.

Smart Queue is your viral post safety net. Instagram limits how many messages can be sent per hour. When your post goes viral and hundreds of comments pour in, a free plan simply can't respond to the overflow — those messages are lost.

With Smart Queue (Pro & Creator plans), overflow messages are automatically saved and processed as soon as capacity frees up. You never miss a single follower, lead, or sale — even during your biggest viral moments.
