Frequently Asked Questions

A DM Flow is an automated conversation sequence. When someone comments on your post with a trigger keyword, the flow automatically:
  • Replies to their comment
  • Sends them a DM with your message
  • Shows interactive buttons for them to tap
  • Continues the conversation based on their responses
Example: Someone comments "LINK" on your Reel → They get a comment reply + a DM with Quick Reply buttons → They tap "Get Free Guide" → They receive your download link!

You can have 1 automated flow running at a time. That flow can be as complex as you want - with multiple steps, Quick Replies, Follower Checks, etc. And it handles unlimited responses!

Want to run automation on multiple posts? Upgrade to Pro (5 flows) or Creator (unlimited flows).

No! Replies are unlimited on all plans. Whether you're on Free, Pro, or Creator - every comment that triggers your flow gets the full automated experience. The only difference between plans is how many flows can be active simultaneously.

Quick Replies are tappable buttons that appear below your DM message. Users can tap them to continue the conversation - perfect for surveys, quizzes, or guiding users to different content.

Button Templates are similar but can include URL buttons that open links directly. Great for directing users to your website, Calendly, or product pages.

Follower Check lets you create different paths based on whether someone follows you or not.

Example: If they're a follower → Send exclusive discount. If not → Ask them to follow first, then send the regular offer.
